# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Household composters, gardeners, and eco-conscious web players looking for a quick, interactive way to learn waste sorting rules.

## Product Purpose
To turn passive browser players into active Android app installers using a gamified web teaser (frictionless browser gameplay) and a secure, offline-enabled Android companion app, while building long-term learning habits.

## Brand Personality
Warm, engaging, trustworthy. Focuses on organic greens and vibrant amber/gold accents (`GARDENGOLD99` theme), polished typography, and smooth micro-interactions that feel premium and alive.

## Anti-references
- Generic corporate SaaS/tech clichés (such as ghost-cards with thin borders and soft, wide drop shadows).
- Low-contrast or washed-out gray-on-white body and placeholder text.
- Amateurish hand-drawn/sketchy SVG doodles.
- Overly rounded corners (32px+) on cards and sections.

## Design Principles
- **Earned Visual Reward:** Interface changes, skin unlocks, and theme shifts must feel substantial and delightful (e.g., amber-gold transition).
- **Security through Simplicity:** Establish instant device safety and privacy reassurance via a zero-permission handshake.
- **Micro-interactivity over Monologue:** Educate through progressive locked states, feedback on mistakes, and streak-saves, not flat text screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Strict adherence to WCAG AA standards:
- Contrast ratio >= 4.5:1 for body and placeholder text.
- Clean responsive layout without text overflow on any viewport.
- Full support for `prefers-reduced-motion` with soft transitions or crossfades.
- Semantic HTML and proper ARIA labels.
